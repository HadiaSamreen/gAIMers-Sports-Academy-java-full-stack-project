OUR STORY

We’re sports enthusiasts.
Sports is everything for us; our teacher, our first love, our driving force. And as sports lovers, we know what it’s like to push yourself to work hard, play better, go against the odds & give it your best shot – all to succeed in something so close to your heart.
We understand how important it is for you to connect with a credible coach who guides you & grooms you. We believe, that a sport isn’t just a hobby. It’s about character building, it’s about learning to focus, it’s about believing in yourself & about giving it your all.
It has shaped us to be the people who we are today: gAIMers.
And we aim to change the way people look at sports, to organise & redefine the sports industry in India, to create a strong base of skilled coaches & to bring out the person in you who’s inspired to get there on the field. You are our team.
And together, we hope to start a revolution that’ll bring together passion & talent, inherent in each and every one of us.
